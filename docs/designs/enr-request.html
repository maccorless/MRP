ENR Request Submission

Submit your prioritised list of Extended Non-Rights Broadcaster (ENR) organisations to the IOC. The IOC will grant or deny allocations from the ENR holdback pool.

How ENR works: Unlike EoI applications (where media orgs apply directly), ENR requests are submitted by your NOC on behalf of ENR organisations. Rank your list by priority — the IOC will work down the list when allocating from the ENR pool. ENR quota is completely separate from your press and photo quotas.
