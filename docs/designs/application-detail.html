About the Organization

The Associated Press is an American not-for-profit news agency headquartered in New York City. Founded in 1846, AP operates 248 bureaus in 99 countries. AP has covered every Olympic Games since 1912, providing wire copy, photography, and video content to member news organizations worldwide. For LA 2028, AP plans to deploy approximately 120 journalists and photographers for comprehensive coverage of all sports and ceremonies.
